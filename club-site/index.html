Welcome to Durham University Mountaineering Club!

About Us

We are a friendly and inclusive club who are very welcoming to all climbers, both experienced and beginners. Come along to our talk on 13/10 to find out more!

Meets

We run day-meets to local crags every weekend, as well as residential meets once or twice a term. These include Peak Meet, Winter Meet, Grit Gathering, and Summer Meet, a week long trip to somewhere further afield after exams.
The club also meets unofficially at the local climbing centre on Mondays and Wednesdays at around 7pm.

Competitions

The Team Captain pays attention to any competitions which may be going on and will post on the facebook group for anyone who wishes to go. We also attend the BUCS Championship every year, the team for which is chosen with an internal competiton in second term.
